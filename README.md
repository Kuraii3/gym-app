# Minimalist Gym Journal 🏋️‍♂️

A lightweight, mobile-first web application designed to replace your physical gym notebook. Built for speed and simplicity, this app helps you execute your workout routines without the distraction of complex charts or bloated features. 

## ✨ Features
* **Custom Workout Splits:** Easily create and edit your own routines (e.g., Upper Body, Lower Body, Push/Pull/Legs).
* **Focused Execution:** Opens directly to a checklist-style interface for your active workout.
* **Simple Logging:** Input your current weight and reps for each exercise.
* **Form & Progress Notes:** Dedicated text areas for each exercise to jot down cues, form corrections, or how the set felt.
* **100% Offline & Private:** No backend, no accounts, and no loading screens. All data is saved locally on your device using the browser's `localStorage`.

## 💡 How to Use
1. Open the app and create a new Split (e.g., "Upper Body").
2. Add your list of exercises to that split.
3. Next time you're at the gym, tap "Start Workout" on your chosen split.
4. Go down the checklist, log your weights/reps, add notes, and hit Finish. Your data will be saved locally for your next session!
